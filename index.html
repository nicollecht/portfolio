About Me

My introduction

Hi, I’m Nicoleta Chitanu — a junior graphic designer with a passion for clean, thoughtful, and purposeful design. My work includes branding, digital graphics, and illustration, shaped by three years of experience as a tattoo artist and strong skills in tools like Procreate. I work with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, and Canva to bring ideas to life, combining creativity with precision. I also have a background in frontend development from STEP IT Academy and a growing interest in UI/UX. Currently, I’m refining my graphic design skills at Tekwill Academy and looking forward to applying them in real-world projects soon.
